The Township Waste Crisis

Inefficient waste management leads to environmental degradation, health hazards, and economic loss in South African townships.

Inefficient Collection

Waste trucks follow fixed routes regardless of bin status, wasting fuel and missing full bins while visiting empty ones.

Illegal Dumping Sites

Unreliable collection schedules lead to overflowing bins and illegal dumping, creating health and environmental hazards.

Lost Recyclable Value

Valuable plastics and packaging get buried in general waste instead of entering the circular economy.

Our Smart Solution

WasteWise Connect uses technology to optimize waste collection and create economic value from recyclables.

Household Reporting

Residents report full bins and presorted recyclables via free USSD code. Earn digital "green points" for participation.

AI-Optimized Routing

Our algorithm creates dynamic routes based on real-time reports, traffic, and recyclable hotspots. Saves 30%+ in fuel costs.

Real-Time Tracking

Communities see truck locations via app. Municipalities monitor operations through a comprehensive dashboard.

Circular Economy

Collected recyclables are sold to processing plants. Revenue funds community rewards and platform sustainability.
